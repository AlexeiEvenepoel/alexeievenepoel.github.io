Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

The LadyFragile Games pipeline ("the Service") runs locally on your own machine. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers except as required by the connected platforms.

2. Platform Authentication

The Service stores OAuth tokens locally on your machine to authenticate with connected platforms (TikTok, YouTube, etc.). These tokens are never shared with third parties.

3. Game Data

Game metadata (Steam URLs, download links, descriptions) is processed locally and used to generate content. This data is sent to the respective platforms only when you choose to publish.

4. Third-Party Services

The Service may communicate with:

  • TikTok — for video publishing (via Content Posting API)
  • YouTube — for video publishing (via YouTube Data API)
  • Netlify — for web deployment
  • Telegram — for posting notifications
  • ShrinkMe / Brave — for URL shortening

Each service has its own privacy policy governing data handling.

5. Data Retention

Generated content (videos, banners, pages) is stored locally until you choose to delete it. Published content on third-party platforms is subject to their retention policies.

6. Cookies

The local web dashboard does not use cookies. Third-party platforms may set their own cookies as governed by their policies.

7. Your Rights

Since the Service runs locally and does not collect data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. For data held by third-party platforms, refer to their respective policies.

8. Changes

We may update this policy at any time. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For questions, contact the repository owner at GitHub: github.com/ladyfragile
